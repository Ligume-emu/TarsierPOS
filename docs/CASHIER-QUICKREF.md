# Cashier Quick Reference

One page. Keep near the terminal.

---

## Starting the day

1. **Turn on the UPS**, then the POS box. Wait for the screen to show TarsierPOS (about 30 seconds).
2. **Log in** with your username and PIN.
3. **Open your shift**: tap *Open Shift*, enter the cash you're starting with, confirm.
4. Print a test receipt to check the printer has paper and ink. Tap *Settings → Test Print*.
5. You're ready to take orders.

---

## Ending the day

1. Finish any pending orders. Do not start new ones after closing time.
2. Tap *Close Shift*.
3. Count the cash in your drawer and enter the total when asked.
4. The system prints an **unofficial Z report**. Keep this with the daily cash.
5. Tap *Logout*. Power off the POS box, then the UPS.

---

## If something goes wrong

### Printer jam (paper stuck)
1. Stop printing.
2. Open the printer lid, gently pull the paper out, clear any scraps.
3. Close the lid until it clicks.
4. Tap *Reprint Last Receipt*.

### Receipt didn't print, no jam
1. Check there's paper in the printer.
2. Tap *Settings → Test Print*. If it prints, retry the receipt.
3. If Test Print also fails: **write the receipt by hand**, note the order number from the POS, and continue serving. Tell Ralph when reachable.

### Screen frozen or app unresponsive
1. Wait 30 seconds — it may catch up.
2. If still stuck, power-cycle the POS box only (not the UPS): hold the power button until it shuts off, then turn back on.
3. The system will come back to the login screen. Log in and continue. Your shift is still open.

### POS box won't turn on at all
1. Power-cycle the UPS (turn off, wait 10 seconds, turn back on).
2. Turn the POS box back on.
3. If still nothing, **fall back to manual receipts** and call Ralph.

### Internet is down and you can't reach Ralph
1. TarsierPOS works without internet — keep using it normally.
2. If TarsierPOS itself is broken too, **fall back to manual receipts**.
3. When the system is back, enter the missed transactions retroactively (or let Ralph reconcile).
