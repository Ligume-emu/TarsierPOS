Notes for implementers

• Width: 32 chars hard. Use RECEIPT_WIDTH = 32 in receipt_service.py (currently 42, sized for 80mm).
• Item rows: when name + " " + qty_price + total exceeds 32 chars, the qty_price block wraps to the next line under the name (see “Chicken Sandwich” in the cash sample).
• Variant lines: indent 2 spaces, show +amount or -amount per price_modifier (ISSUE-074 negative supported).
• VAT-inc subtotal label: only when vat_enabled OR is_vat_exempt (FLAG-064 parity rule).
• BIR fields: TIN exists on BusinessProfile; MIN and Serial need new fields (FLAG-056 follow-up — render placeholders for now).
• Z-report: Reset counter, beginning/ending OR#, accumulated grand total, and BIR Permit # are mandatory; design here is what FEATURE-011 (B12) must finalize against.
• Parity: On-screen receipt (success modal) renders the same fields and ordering. Diverges → FLAG-064 reopens.
