5 · Dialogs & toasts (ISSUE-085 spec)

One confirm(), one alert(), one toast(). Retires the five duplicate implementations cataloged in AUDIT-005 §3. Confirm returns Promise<boolean>.

5.1 Confirm — normal

Clear cart?

This removes all items from the current sale. The customer record is not affected.

5.2 Confirm — danger

Void this sale?

Sale #1042 (₱315.00) will be voided. This action is logged and cannot be undone.

5.3 Alert

Receipt printer offline

The thermal printer at 192.168.1.87 didn't respond. The sale was saved; you can reprint from the X-report screen.

5.4 Toasts — info / success / warning / danger

ℹ️ Inventory synced (12 items updated)
✅ Sale #1042 completed — ₱315.00
⚠️ Iced Latte stock low (3 left)
❌ Payment gateway timed out — retry

Position: bottom-right. Duration: 3500ms. Max 3 stacked. (AUDIT-005 Decision 10.)
